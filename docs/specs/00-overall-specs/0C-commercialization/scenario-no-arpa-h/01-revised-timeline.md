# Revised Timeline: Without ARPA-H Funding

## Executive Summary

This timeline assumes **no ARPA-H funding**. The strategy pivots to:
1. **Wellness-first** launch (unregulated)
2. **CDS exemption** path for clinical capabilities
3. **VC-first** funding model
4. **Slower but viable** 3-year plan

## Summary

Without ARPA-H, the path is **slower but achievable**:

- **Year 1**: Wellness + open-source foundation ($500K-750K revenue)
- **Year 2**: CDS clinical expansion ($3-5M revenue)
- **Year 3**: FDA clearance + platform ($15-25M revenue)

vs. With ARPA-H:
- Year 1: $3.3M
- Year 2: $12M
- Year 3: $50M

**Total delay**: ~12 months to FDA, ~50% revenue reduction in Year 3

The trade-off: More equity retained, more market-driven execution, less government compliance overhead.
