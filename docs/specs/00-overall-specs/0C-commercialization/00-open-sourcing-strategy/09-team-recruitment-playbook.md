# Team Recruitment Playbook

## Overview

This document provides step-by-step instructions for recruiting three critical team members before the Solution Summary deadline (February 27, 2026).

| Role | Priority | Deadline | Impact on Chances |
|------|----------|----------|-------------------|
| **US Cardiologist (HF specialist)** | CRITICAL | Feb 15, 2026 | +10% |
| **FDA SaMD Consultant** | HIGH | Feb 20, 2026 | +5% |
| **Health System Innovation Leader** | MEDIUM | Feb 25, 2026 | TA3 intros |

**Combined impact:** 65-75% chance of Full Proposal invitation (Stage 1)

**Two-stage process:**
- **Stage 1 (Solution Summary → Full Proposal):** ~10-15 teams invited, we're at 65-75% with full package
- **Stage 2 (Full Proposal → Final 5):** 5 finalists, 33-50% depends on TA3 partner quality
- **Overall to Final 5:** 22-38%

**Why these numbers:** ARPA-H uses a wider funnel at Solution Summary stage. Our testable demo (50% MVP) + MIS dashboard + open-source TA2 stack (unique among applicants) + complete RCT protocol are our main differentiators. The real competition is at Full Proposal stage where TA3 partner quality matters most.
